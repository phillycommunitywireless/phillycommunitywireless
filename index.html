Philly Community Wireless is building community-controlled wireless internet networks in Philadelphia. We aim to provide a baseline of internet access for everyone, enhancing quality of life today and positioning communities for a more healthy and prosperous tomorrow.
What We Do

Our community wireless network currently exists in North Philadelphia, with a focus on Norris Square, Fairhill, and Kensington. If you live in the 19122, 19133, or 19134 zip codes, reach out! Check out our map for more details on where you can find us and get connected.
